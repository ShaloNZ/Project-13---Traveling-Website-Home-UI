As you embark on your journey across Sri Lanka, you'll discover a mosaic of vibrant landscapes, from pristine beaches with golden sands to lush tea plantations that blanket our hill country. The diversity of our natural scenery is matched only by the warmth of our people, who are eager to share the wonders of their homeland with you. Sri Lanka's rich history spans over 2,500 years, and this is reflected in our ancient cities, magnificent temples, and timeless traditions.
